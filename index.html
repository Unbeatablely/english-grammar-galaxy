名词分类 Classification

可数不可数专有普通抽象集合物质🗣️ I have two books and some water. London is a bustling city.📝 The research provides new evidence about happiness.

's所有格of所有格双重所有格🗣️ Tom's car; the leg of the table; a friend of my father's📝 The company's quarterly report; the findings of the study

冠词 Articles — a / an / the / Ø

← 返回星系

不定冠词 a / an Indefinite Article

表泛指类指首次提及=one🗣️ I saw a dog in the park. She is an engineer. (元音音素前用an)📝 A balanced diet is essential for health. (类指：任何均衡饮食)
🗣️日常📝学术
📋 考试：专四 a用于辅音音素前（a university /ju:/），an用于元音音素前（an hour /aʊə/）；固定搭配：have a cold, take a walk, make a difference

定冠词 the Definite Article

特指唯一事物序数词/最高级前江河海洋乐器🗣️ The sun rises in the east. She plays the piano.📝 The results of the experiment were published in The Lancet.
🗣️日常📝学术
📋 考试：专四 the + 形容词表一类人（the poor/rich/elderly→复数）；the + 姓氏复数表一家人（the Smiths）

零冠词 Ø Zero Article

不使用冠词的场景：学科球类三餐季节月份泛指复数🗣️ I study chemistry. We play basketball after lunch in summer.📝 Dogs are loyal animals. (泛指复数，不加the)
🗣️日常
📋 考试：专四 固定零冠词：go to school/bed/hospital；by bus/bike/train；at night/noon/dawn

一般现在时 Simple Present

经常性动作、客观事实。do/does动词原形/三单🗣️ I walk to school. The sun rises in the east.📝 The study confirms the hypothesis.
🗣️日常📝学术

一般过去时 Simple Past

过去完成的动作。did动词过去式🗣️ She visited Paris. They were happy.✍️ He walked into the room and sat down.
🗣️日常✍️文学

一般将来时 Simple Future

will+Vbe going tobe to dobe about to🗣️ I will call you. It is going to rain. The train is about to leave.
🗣️日常

现在进行时 Present Continuous

am/is/are+V-ing🗣️ She is reading now. I am meeting him later. (安排好的将来)
🗣️日常

过去进行时 Past Continuous

was/were+V-ing🗣️ I was sleeping when you called. They were playing at 3pm.
🗣️日常

将来进行时 Future Continuous

will be+V-ing🗣️ This time tomorrow I will be flying to London.
🗣️日常

现在完成时 Present Perfect

have/has+过去分词🗣️ I have finished. She has lived here since 2010.📝 Researchers have identified three key factors.
🗣️日常📝学术

过去完成时 Past Perfect

将来完成时 Future Perfect

will have+过去分词🗣️ By next year, I will have graduated.
🗣️日常

现在完成进行时 Present Perfect Continuous

have/has been+V-ing🗣️ I have been waiting for two hours!
🗣️日常

过去完成进行时 Past Perfect Continuous

had been+V-ing🗣️ He had been working there for 10 years before he quit.
🗣️日常

将来完成进行时 Future Perfect Continuous

will have been+V-ing🗣️ By June, I will have been studying here for 3 years.

主动 vs 被动 Active vs Passive

一般现在 am/is/are done 一般过去 was/were done 一般将来 will be done 现在进行 is/are being done 过去进行 was/were being done 现在完成 have/has been done 过去完成 had been done 情态 can/must be done

主动表被动 Active Form, Passive Meaning

句子成分 Sentence Elements

← 返回星系

主语 Subject

句子的主体。可作主语：名词代词动名词不定式名词从句🗣️ The sun rises. Swimming is fun. What you said is true.

谓语 Predicate

说明主语的动作或状态。简单谓语 She runs. 复合谓语 She can speak English. He is reading.📝 The theory has been widely accepted.

宾语 Object

直接宾语 动作直接承受者 间接宾语 动作间接对象 介词宾语🗣️ She gave me (间) a book (直). I'm thinking about you (介).

表语&补语 Predicative & Complement

主补(表语) He is a teacher. She looks tired. 宾补 We elected him president. They painted the wall white.

定语 Attribute

修饰名词或代词。前置 a beautiful flower 后置 the girl in red; the book on the desk; the man who came

状语 Adverbial

修饰动词/形容词/副词/全句。时间地点方式原因目的结果条件程度频率让步🗣️ She arrived yesterday. He ran quickly. To pass, you must study.

主谓一致 Subject-Verb Agreement

← 返回星系

语法一致 Grammatical Concord

主语单复数决定动词形式。🗣️ The cat sleeps. / The cats sleep. Each student has a book.📋 考试：专四 everyone/someone/nobody/each/either/neither→单数动词；both/few/many/several→复数

意义一致 Notional Concord

根据实际意义决定动词数。🗣️ The team are divided. (成员→复数) / The team is the best. (整体→单数)📝 The news is shocking. The police have arrived.
🗣️日常📝学术

就近原则 Proximity

动词与最近的主语一致。either…orneither…nornot only…but alsothere be🗣️ Neither the students nor the teacher was present. There is a book and two pens.

特殊结构 Special Cases

a number of+复数→复数 the number of→单数 分数/百分比取决于of后名词📝 70% of the water is polluted. 70% of students are present.

倒装句 Inversion

← 返回星系

完全倒装 Full Inversion

整个谓语在主语前。🗣️ Here comes the bus! There goes my chance.✍️ On the hill stood an ancient castle. In the distance lay a vast desert.
🗣️日常✍️文学

部分倒装 Partial Inversion

助动词/情态动词提前。否定词/only放句首触发。🗣️ Never have I seen such beauty! Not until then did I realize the truth.📝 Only by working together can we solve this problem.
🗣️日常📝学术
📋 考试：专八 否定副词句首必倒装：Never/Seldom/Rarely/Hardly…when/No sooner…than/Under no circumstances

as/though 倒装 As/Though Inversion

表语/状语提前到as/though前表让步。✍️ Tired as he was, he kept working. Try as he might, he failed.🗣️ Much as I like it, I can't afford it.
✍️文学🗣️日常

强调句 Emphatic Sentences

← 返回星系

It is/was...that... Cleft Sentences

强调除谓语外的任何成分。🗣️ It was Tom that broke the window. It was yesterday that I met her.📝 It is through practice that one masters a language.📋 考试：专八 判定方法：去掉It is/was…that后句子仍完整→强调句；不完整→主语从句

其他强调方式 Other Emphasis Methods

do/does/did+V原形 强调谓语：I do believe you.
not…until强调 It was not until midnight that he returned.
what从句 What I need is a break.
倒装强调 见倒装句章节🗣️ I did finish it! (口语中强调"确实"做完了)
🗣️日常

省略句 Ellipsis

← 返回星系

状语从句省略 Ellipsis in Adverbials

当从句主语与主句主语一致，且从句含be动词时，可省略主语+be。🗣️ When (he was) asked, he refused. If (it is) possible, call me.📝 While (they were) conducting the experiment, the researchers noted…
🗣️日常📝学术

不定式省略 Infinitive Ellipsis

保留to，省略重复的动词部分。🗣️ Would you like to come? — I'd love to. You can go if you want to.💼 Please submit the report if you are able to.
🗣️日常💼商务

that省略 & 对话省略 That Omission & Conversational

宾语从句that可省（口语/非正式）；对话中主语/助动词常省略。🗣️ I know (that) you're right. (You) Coming? (I'll) See you later!📝 The study found (that) the effect was significant. (学术中也常见)
🗣️日常📝学术

There be 句型 There-be Structure

← 返回星系

基本结构与时态 Basic Structure & Tenses

一般现在 There is/are 一般过去 There was/were 一般将来 There will be 完成时 There have/has been 情态 There can/must be🗣️ There is a book on the desk. There have been many changes.📝 There appears to be a correlation. (There + seem/appear/happen + to be)
🗣️日常📝学术

主谓一致与变体 Agreement & Variants

动词与最近的主语一致（就近原则）。变体：There exist/lie/live/stand/remain🗣️ There is a pen and two books. (就近：a pen→单数)✍️ There lived an old man in the village. There stands a temple on the hill.
🗣️日常✍️文学

It 的用法 Uses of "It"

← 返回星系

人称代词 it Personal Pronoun

指代前文提到的事物、动物、婴儿（性别不明时）、天气、时间、距离。🗣️ Where's my phone? — It's on the table. It's raining. It's 5pm.🗣️ It's a long way to Tipperary. It's a boy! (新生儿)
🗣️日常

形式主语·形式宾语 Dummy Subject & Object

用it代替真正的主语/宾语（不定式/动名词/从句），避免句子头重脚轻。🗣️ It is important to exercise. I find it hard to believe.📝 It has been suggested that climate change is accelerating.
🗣️日常📝学术
📋 考试：专四专八 形式宾语结构：make/find/consider/think + it + adj./n. + to do/that从句

情态动词 Modal Verbs

← 返回星系

能力与许可 Ability & Permission

can/couldmay/mightbe able to🗣️ I can swim. May I come in? I was able to finish it. (特定过去能力)

义务与必要 Obligation & Necessity

must/have toshould/ought toneed/dare🗣️ You must go. (主观) / You have to go. (客观) / You needn't rush.

推测 Speculation

must be 肯定(95%) should be 应该(70%) may/might/could be 可能(30-50%) can't be 不可能(0%)🗣️ He must be at home. She may have missed the bus.

情态+完成时 Modal + Perfect

must have done 一定做过 could have done 本可做 should have done 本应做 needn't have done 本不必做📋 考试：专四 need have done仅用于否定和疑问；didn't need to do(不必且没做) vs needn't have done(不必但做了)

半情态动词 Semi-Modals

used to 过去常常 be supposed to 按理应该 had better 最好 would rather 宁愿🗣️ I used to smoke. You had better not be late.

比较结构 Comparison Structures

← 返回星系

原级&比较级 Positive & Comparative

as…as 和…一样 not as/so…as 不如 -er than/more…than🗣️ She is as tall as me. This is better than that.📝 The more you practice, the better you become.
🗣️日常📝学术

最高级 Superlative

the -est / the most+原级 + 比较范围🗣️ She is the smartest in class. This is the most important issue.
🗣️日常📝学术

倍数表达 Multiples

twice/three times + as…as …times + 比较级 + than …times + the size/length of🗣️ This room is twice as big as that one. This house is three times the size of ours.
🗣️日常💼商务

特殊比较结构 Special Patterns

no more than 仅仅 no less than 多达 more A than B 与其B不如A not so much A as B 与其A不如B🗣️ He is more lucky than clever. (与其说聪明，不如说运气好)📋 考试：专四 比较对象要对等：His salary is higher than mine (≠ than me)
