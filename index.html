词性 Parts of Speech

← 返回星系

名词 Nouns

表示人、事物、地点或抽象概念。分为可数不可数专有普通抽象集合 🗣️ The book on the table holds great wisdom. 📝 The analysis of data reveals hidden patterns.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
"What's in a name? That which we call a rose by any other name would smell as sweet."— William Shakespeare, Romeo and Juliet

动词 Verbs

表示动作或状态。分为及物 vt.不及物 vi.系动词助动词情态动词 🗣️ She runs every morning and is full of energy. 💼 We launched the product and secured major contracts. ✍️ The wind howled through the empty streets.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务✍️ 文学
"I came, I saw, I conquered."— Julius Caesar (attrib.)

形容词 Adjectives

修饰名词或代词，表示性质、特征。有原级比较级最高级变化。 🗣️ That's a beautiful dress! — This one is more elegant. 📝 The results were significant; the most compelling finding was… ✍️ It was a dark and stormy night, bleak and unforgiving.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术✍️ 文学
"It was the best of times, it was the worst of times."— Charles Dickens, A Tale of Two Cities

副词 Adverbs

修饰动词、形容词、其他副词或整个句子。方式程度频率时间地点 🗣️ She sings beautifully and works very hard every day. 📝 The experiment was carefully designed and rigorously executed. 💼 We urgently need to significantly reduce costs.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"Slowly and surely, the tide inexorably rose."— Adapted from Agatha Christie

代词 Pronouns

代替名词的词。人称物主反身指示不定疑问关系 🗣️ She gave her book to me. This one is mine. 📝 One must consider all the evidence before one concludes.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
"To thine own self be true, and it must follow, as the night the day, thou canst not then be false to any man."— Shakespeare, Hamlet

介词 Prepositions

表示名词/代词与其他词的关系。时间地点方向方式原因 🗣️ The cat is on the roof at midnight. I'm going to the store. 📝 According to the report, profits rose by 15% during Q3. ✍️ Beyond the hills, across the river, lies a hidden valley.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务✍️ 文学
"In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth."— Genesis 1:1, King James Bible

连词 Conjunctions

连接词、短语或句子。并列 and/but/or从属 because/if/when关联 both…and 🗣️ I like tea and coffee, but I prefer water. 📝 Although the sample was small, the results were significant. 💼 Neither the CEO nor the CFO was available for comment.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"And in the end, the love you take is equal to the love you make."— Paul McCartney, The Beatles

冠词 · 感叹词 Articles & Interjections

不定冠词 a/an定冠词 the零冠词 —— 感叹词表达情绪：Oh! Wow! Alas! 🗣️ A dog chased the cat. Wow! That's amazing! 📝 The universe is expanding. An electron is a fundamental particle. ✍️ Alas! Poor Yorick! I knew him, Horatio.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术✍️ 文学
"The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step."— Laozi (attrib.)

时态体系 Tenses — 12 种核心时态

← 返回星系

一般现在时 Simple Present

经常性动作、客观事实、普遍真理。do/does动词原形/三单 🗣️ I walk to school every day. She drinks tea, not coffee. 📝 The Earth revolves around the Sun. Water boils at 100°C. 💼 Our company serves over 10,000 clients worldwide.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"The sun also rises, and the sun goes down."— Ecclesiastes 1:5

一般过去时 Simple Past

过去发生的动作或状态。did动词过去式不规则变化 🗣️ She visited Paris last year. They were so happy together. ✍️ He walked into the room and sat down without a word. 📝 In 1928, Fleming discovered penicillin by accident.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学📝 学术
"I had a dream."— Martin Luther King Jr., abridged

一般将来时 Simple Future

将来发生的动作或预测。will + Vbe going tobe about to 🗣️ I will call you tomorrow. Look at those clouds — it is going to rain. 💼 We will launch the new product next quarter. The deal is about to close. ✍️ Tomorrow, and tomorrow, and tomorrow… Life will go on.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务✍️ 文学
"I shall return."— General Douglas MacArthur

现在进行时 Present Continuous

正在进行的动作或近期安排。am/is/are + V-ing 🗣️ She is reading a book right now. I am meeting him at 6. 💼 We are currently reviewing your application. 📝 The climate is changing at an unprecedented rate.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务📝 学术

过去进行时 Past Continuous

过去某时刻正在进行的动作。was/were + V-ing 🗣️ I was sleeping when you called. They were playing football at 3pm. ✍️ The rain was falling steadily as she was walking home alone.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学

将来进行时 Future Continuous

将来某时刻正在进行的动作。will be + V-ing 🗣️ This time tomorrow I will be flying to London. 💼 We will be discussing the budget at 2pm tomorrow.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务

现在完成时 Present Perfect

过去动作对现在的影响或持续到现在。have/has + 过去分词 🗣️ I have finished my homework. She has lived here since 2010. 📝 Researchers have identified three key factors. 💼 We have seen a 20% increase in sales this quarter.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"I have been young, and now am old; yet have I not seen the righteous forsaken."— Psalm 37:25 📋 考试聚焦：
专四 TEM-4 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时是高频考点——关键词区分：since/for / already / yet / ever / never / recently / so far / in the past few years → 用现在完成时；yesterday / last week / in 2010 / …ago → 用一般过去时
雅思 IELTS Writing Task 1 描述近期趋势："Sales have increased steadily over the past decade." Speaking 中展示语法多样性时，现在完成时是不可或缺的时态之一

过去完成时 Past Perfect

表示"过去的过去"。had + 过去分词 🗣️ By the time I arrived, the movie had already started. ✍️ She realized she had left her keys in the door. The thief had entered while she slept. 📝 The subjects had shown no prior symptoms before the treatment began.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学📝 学术
📋 考试聚焦：
专八 TEM-8 阅读理解和翻译题中的叙事时间线判断——过去完成时标记"过去的过去"，常与 by the time / before / when / after 引导的时间状语从句搭配。改错题中注意与一般过去时的区分：两个过去的动作，先发生的用过去完成时

将来完成时 Future Perfect

将来某时之前已完成的动作。will have + 过去分词 🗣️ By next year, I will have graduated from university. 💼 By Q4, we will have completed the merger process.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务

现在完成进行时 Present Perfect Continuous

从过去持续到现在，强调持续性。have/has been + V-ing 🗣️ I have been waiting for two hours! Where have you been? 📝 Scientists have been studying this phenomenon for decades.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术

过去完成进行时 Past Perfect Continuous

过去某时之前一直进行的动作。had been + V-ing 🗣️ He had been working there for 10 years before he finally quit. ✍️ The detective had been following the suspect for weeks before making the arrest.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学

将来完成进行时 Future Perfect Continuous

持续到将来某时的动作。will have been + V-ing 🗣️ By June, I will have been studying here for exactly three years. 💼 By the end of this project, our team will have been collaborating for 18 months.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务

语态 Voice

← 返回星系

主动语态 Active Voice

主语是动作的执行者。直接、有力、清晰。 🗣️ The chef cooks the meal with great care. 📝 The researcher conducted three experiments to verify the hypothesis. 💼 Our team delivered the project ahead of schedule.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"I have nothing to offer but blood, toil, tears and sweat."— Winston Churchill (active, first-person)

被动语态 Passive Voice

主语是动作的承受者。结构：be + 过去分词 (+ by 施动者) 🗣️ The meal was cooked by a famous chef. 📝 The experiment was conducted under controlled conditions. 💼 The contract was signed by both parties yesterday.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"We hold these truths to be self-evident, that all men are created equal."— Declaration of Independence 📋 考试聚焦：
雅思 IELTS Writing Task 1 图表描述大量使用被动："It can be seen that…" / "A significant increase was observed in…" / "The figure is projected to reach…" 被动语态是雅思学术写作 Task 1 的必备技能。
专四 TEM-4 各时态被动语态转换（尤其完成时和进行时被动）、不及物动词无被动（happen / appear / occur / take place / break out）

被动语态 — 各时态对照 Passive Across Tenses

一般现在 am/is/are done
一般过去 was/were done
一般将来 will be done
现在进行 am/is/are being done
过去进行 was/were being done
现在完成 have/has been done
过去完成 had been done
情态动词 can/must/should be done 🗣️ The window was broken (by the storm). — 施动者不明确时用被动 📝 In academic writing, passive voice is common: "It has been shown that…"
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
🎭

语气 Mood

← 返回星系

陈述语气 Indicative Mood

陈述事实、观点或提出问题。最常用的语气，涵盖绝大多数日常交流。 🗣️ The Earth orbits the Sun. Do you like coffee? 📝 The study demonstrates a clear correlation between diet and health. 💼 Our revenue exceeded projections for the third consecutive quarter.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务

祈使语气 Imperative Mood

表达命令、请求、建议、警告。通常省略主语 you，直接用动词原形。 🗣️ Open the door, please. Don't be late! Let's go! 💼 Please find the report attached. Do not hesitate to contact us. ✍️ To be, or not to be… (不定式也可表祈使)
🗣️ 日常💼 商务✍️ 文学
"Ask not what your country can do for you — ask what you can do for your country."— John F. Kennedy

虚拟语气 Subjunctive Mood

表达愿望、假设、建议、要求等非事实情况。英语中最易被忽略的语气。

▸ 与现在相反：If I were…
If I were you, I would accept the offer. (但我不是你)
▸ 与过去相反：If I had done…
If she had studied harder, she would have passed. (但她当时没努力)
▸ 与将来可能相反：If I were to… / should…
If it were to rain tomorrow, the event would be canceled.
▸ wish 后的虚拟：
I wish I were taller. I wish I had known the truth earlier.
▸ 建议/要求/命令后的 that 从句：(动词原形)
I suggest that he go immediately. It is essential that she be present.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术💼 商务
"If music be the food of love, play on."— Shakespeare, Twelfth Night 📋 考试聚焦：
专四 TEM-4 条件句三种基本虚拟（与现在/过去/将来相反）、wish 后的虚拟、as if/though、it's (high) time that… did、would rather that… did
专八 TEM-8 含蓄虚拟（but for / without / otherwise / or）、错综时间条件句（从句过去 + 主句现在）、名词性从句中的虚拟（suggest/order/require/insist/recommend that sb. (should) do）
雅思 IELTS Speaking Part 3 表达假设与推测（If I were to speculate… / I would imagine that…）、Writing Task 2 让步假设（Even if it were the case…）

句子类型 Sentence Types

← 返回星系

简单句 Simple Sentence

一个独立分句，包含一个主语和一个谓语。五大基本句型： S+VS+V+OS+V+CS+V+O+OS+V+O+C 🗣️ The cat slept. — S+V (主谓) 🗣️ I love coffee. — S+V+O (主谓宾) 🗣️ She is a doctor. — S+V+C (主系表) 🗣️ He gave me a gift. — S+V+O+O (主谓双宾) 🗣️ They elected him president. — S+V+O+C (主谓宾补)
🗣️ 日常

并列句 Compound Sentence

两个或多个独立分句由并列连词连接。 FANBOYS: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so 🗣️ I wanted to go, but it was too late, so I stayed home. 💼 The market is volatile, yet our portfolio remains stable. ✍️ The sun was setting, and the sky turned a brilliant shade of crimson.
🗣️ 日常💼 商务✍️ 文学
"It is a far, far better thing that I do, than I have ever done; it is a far, far better rest that I go to than I have ever known."— Dickens, A Tale of Two Cities

复合句 Complex Sentence

一个主句 + 一个或多个从句。从句由从属连词或关系词引导。 becausealthoughifwhenwhichthat 🗣️ Although I was tired, I finished the work that was due. 📝 Because the sample size was limited, further research that involves more participants is needed.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
"When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."— Paulo Coelho, The Alchemist

四大功能句型 By Function

陈述句 Declarative I enjoy learning English.
疑问句 Interrogative Do you enjoy learning English? What do you find most challenging?
祈使句 Imperative Practice English every single day.
感叹句 Exclamatory How beautiful this language is! What a journey it has been! 💼 Could you please send the documents? (疑问表请求) 🗣️ What an incredible performance that was! (感叹)
🗣️ 日常💼 商务

从句体系 Clauses

← 返回星系

名词性从句 Noun Clauses

在句中充当名词的角色。引导词：thatwhether/ifwh-词
▸ 主语从句：What you said makes perfect sense. ▸ 宾语从句：I know that he is honest. She asked whether I was available. ▸ 表语从句：The problem is that we lack sufficient funding. ▸ 同位语从句：The fact that he lied shocked everyone in the room.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
"It is a truth universally acknowledged, that a single man in possession of a good fortune, must be in want of a wife."— Jane Austen, Pride and Prejudice (that-同位语从句)

定语从句 Relative Clauses

修饰名词或代词，相当于形容词。关系词：
whowhomwhosewhichthatwhenwherewhy

▸ 限定性（不可缺少）：
The book that I borrowed from you was absolutely fascinating. ▸ 非限定性（补充信息，逗号隔开，不用 that）：
My father, who is a retired doctor, still volunteers at the clinic. Paris, where I spent my childhood, will always be home.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学
"The person, be it gentleman or lady, who has not pleasure in a good novel, must be intolerably stupid."— Jane Austen 📋 考试聚焦：
专四 TEM-4 定语从句是专四语法选择题的必考点：限定性 vs 非限定性（逗号 + which/who，不能用 that）、介词 + which/whom（in which / of whom / to which）、whose 的用法（= of which / of whom）
雅思 IELTS Writing Task 2 高分句式：用 which 引导非限定性定语从句对前文观点进行补充评论，"…, which suggests that…" / "…, which has led to considerable debate." 这能直接提升 Grammatical Range 评分。

状语从句 Adverbial Clauses

修饰动词、形容词或整个句子，表示各种状语关系：
时间 when/while/as/after/before/since/until 地点 where/wherever 原因 because/since/as/now that 目的 so that/in order that/for fear that 结果 so…that/such…that 条件 if/unless/as long as/provided that 让步 although/though/even though/while 方式 as/as if/as though 比较 than/as…as/the more…the more 🗣️ When the rain stopped, we went out because the sun was shining. 📝 Although the data is promising, we cannot draw conclusions until the trial is complete. ✍️ Wherever you go, there you are.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术✍️ 文学
"If you can dream—and not make dreams your master; If you can think—and not make thoughts your aim…"— Rudyard Kipling, "If—" 📋 考试聚焦：
专八 TEM-8 翻译题高频考点——英译汉时状语从句的语序调整（英语状语从句在后，汉语在前）；改错题中 as / though 引导的倒装让步状语从句（"Try as he might, he couldn't solve it." = Although he tried hard…）
雅思 IELTS Writing Task 2 中使用让步状语从句（Although / Even though / While it is true that…）展示辩证思维，是 Band 7+ 的标志性句式

非谓语动词 Non-finite Verbs

← 返回星系

不定式 Infinitive (to + V)

兼有名词、形容词、副词的性质。用途极广。
作主语 To learn a language requires patience.
作宾语 I want to travel the world someday.
作宾补 She asked me to wait for her outside.
作定语 I have a lot of work to finish before Friday.
作状语 He came to help (目的). She is old enough to decide (结果). 📝 To understand quantum mechanics, one must first master linear algebra.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术
"To be, or not to be: that is the question."— Shakespeare, Hamlet

动名词 Gerund (V-ing)

动词的 -ing 形式，起名词作用。
作主语 Swimming is excellent exercise for the whole body.
作宾语 I really enjoy reading historical novels.
作表语 My favorite hobby is painting landscapes.
作定语 a swimming pool, a reading room
只能接动名词的常见动词：enjoyavoidsuggestmindfinishpracticeadmitconsiderdenypostpone 🗣️ Would you mind opening the window? I avoid eating too late at night.
🗣️ 日常

分词 Participles

现在分词 (V-ing)：表示主动/进行
作定语 the sleeping baby, a burning desire
作状语 Walking home, I ran into an old friend.
作补语 I saw him running across the street.

过去分词 (V-ed)：表示被动/完成
作定语 a broken window, the forgotten hero
作状语 Frightened by the noise, the deer ran away.
作补语 I had my hair cut yesterday. ✍️ Exhausted from the journey, she collapsed onto the bed.
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学

不定式 vs. 动名词 Key Differences

意义不同的动词对：
remember to do 记得要去做（未做）
remember doing 记得做过（已做）
forget to do 忘记要做（未做）
forget doing 忘记做过（已做，用于否定句）
stop to do 停下来去做另一件事
stop doing 停止正在做的事
try to do 努力/设法做（困难的事）
try doing 尝试做（看看效果）
regret to do 遗憾地要做（正式通知）
regret doing 后悔做过某事
mean to do 打算做
mean doing 意味着 🗣️ I remember locking the door, but I'll check anyway. (记得锁过了) 🗣️ Please remember to lock the door before you leave. (别忘了锁)
🗣️ 日常
📋 考试聚焦：
专四 TEM-4 非谓语动词是专四语法与词汇部分的第一大考点。forget / remember / regret / stop / try / mean / go on 后接 to do 和 doing 的区别几乎年年出现。建议记忆口诀："forget to do 忘去做，forget doing 忘做过；remember to do 记去做，remember doing 记做过；try to do 努力做，try doing 试试看；mean to do 打算做，mean doing 意味着"
专八 TEM-8 独立主格结构（Nominative Absolute）："Weather permitting, we'll go hiking." / "All things considered, it was a success." 分词完成式 having done / having been done 表示动作先于谓语动词发生

句子成分 Sentence Elements

← 返回星系

主语 Subject

句子的主体，动作的执行者或被描述对象。常由名词代词动名词不定式名词从句充当。 🗣️ The sun rises in the east. — 名词作主语 📝 What the study reveals is alarming. — 名词从句作主语（主语从句） ✍️ To err is human; to forgive, divine. — 不定式作主语
🗣️ 日常📝 学术✍️ 文学

谓语 Predicate

说明主语的动作或状态。由动词充当，是句子的核心。 🗣️ The baby cried. → 简单谓语（一个动词） 🗣️ She can speak three languages. → 复合谓语（情态动词 + 实义动词） 🗣️ He is reading a novel. → 复合谓语（助动词 + 分词） 📝 The theory has been widely accepted by the scientific community.
🗣️ 日常📝 学术

宾语 Object

动作的承受者。直接宾语动作直接对象 间接宾语动作间接对象 介词宾语介词后的宾语 🗣️ She gave me (间宾) a book (直宾). 🗣️ I'm thinking about you. — 介词宾语 💼 Please send the client (间宾) the updated contract (直宾).
🗣️ 日常💼 商务

补语 Complement

补充说明主语或宾语的成分。 主补 (Subject Complement) — 系动词之后
🗣️ He is a brilliant scientist. She looks tired today. 宾补 (Object Complement) — 补充说明宾语
🗣️ We elected him president. They painted the wall white. ✍️ The news left her speechless. — 形容词作宾补
🗣️ 日常✍️ 文学

定语 Attribute

修饰名词或代词的成分。前置定语位于名词前 后置定语位于名词后 🗣️ a beautiful flower — 形容词前置 🗣️ the girl in red — 介词短语后置 🗣️ The book on the desk is mine. — 介词短语后置 📝 The methodology employed in this study is rigorous. — 分词短语后置 ✍️ The house, weathered by centuries of storms, stood proudly on the cliff.

状语 Adverbial
